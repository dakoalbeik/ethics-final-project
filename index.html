Private || Public

Introduction:

With the rise of technology, privacy is becoming a concept of the past. Users are more inclined than ever before to give up their privacy to get their desired level of comfortability. What makes this matter extra critical is that comfortability has no limit, and there is an obvious linear relationship between giving up privacy and having a more comfortable life.

As a magician, I thought of something similar to this. Some magicians claim to read minds. They put on acts of entertainment to demonstrate something that seems impossible. These performers are known as mentalists. Hot reading, a technique used by psychics and mentalists to pretend to predict or know something personal about an audience member.

This technique requires the performer to know some information about the audience member prior to the performance. In this book, I will compare advertisers and technology companies who gather information about their users with unethical mentalists who get information about their audiences prior to the show.
